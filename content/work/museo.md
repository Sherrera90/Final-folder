<h3 class="small_title">Overview</h3>
<p>
  Museo is a new concept that I came up with from start to finish. Through market research, competitive analysis, and user interviews,
  I was able to explore how mobile technology can make museums more accessible to people around the world. I designed a virtual museum
  app that allows users to explore exhibits, tour museums remotely, and discover art and history from their phone. This helps to remove
  barriers such as travel, cost, and accessibility. This gives a wider range of individuals the opportunity to discover new art.
</p>

<div class="content_bottom">
  <h3 class="small_title">Challenge</h3>
  <p>
    Many people want to experience museums, but can't always visit in person. Existing virtual museum experiences often feel static,
    confusing, or difficult to navigate, making it hard for users to stay engaged or explore content meaningfully. Figuring
    out how to get most museums on a single app was complicated due to licensing and storage.
  </p>

  <h3 class="small_title">Solution</h3>
  <div class="portfolio-item_goals">
    <p>
      Design an intuitive and engaging mobile experience that allows users to explore museums from anywhere while making discovery
      feel simple, immersive, and accessible for all users.
    </p>
  </div>
