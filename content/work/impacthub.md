<h3 class="small_title">Overview</h3>
<p>
  ImpactHub is a platform for both individuals who are looking to volunteer and non-profits looking for volunteers.
  The app provides a private space for the user to explore on their own, and gives the organizations a chance to expand their volunteer base. This helps to simplify onboarding, boost engagement, track hours from both sides, all while giving the users an in-depth look into the individuals and charities available.
</p>

<div class="content_bottom">
  <h3 class="small_title">Challenge</h3>
  <p>
    There are many websites out there that are designed to help users find places to volunteer, but they lack functionality, discoverability, or have inconsistent information. Users asked for full disclosure regarding the organization, their policies, and where their funds are being allocated. They also requested volunteer tracking abilities, and organization features like a calendar and hours tracked.
  </p>

  <h3 class="small_title">Solution</h3>
  <div class="portfolio-item_goals">
    <p>
      The goal was to create a mobile app that was functional, and easy to use while addressing all of the needs of our users. Optimizing the user interface for both large and small screens, creating functionality that transitions between devices, and enhancing accessibility to a variety of users.
    </p>

  <h3 class="small_title">Summary</h3>
  <p>
    Through thoughtful research, transparent data, and intuitive design, this app creates a simple and
    trustworthy way for individuals to connect with nonprofits. Features like the volunteer calendar, rating
    system, and funding breakdown were directly informed by real user needs and helped shape the overall experience. Working through the full process,
    from research to wireframes, strengthened my ability to think critically and design with empathy and accessibility.
    I was able to translate research into thoughtful design decisions and create clear solutions that supported user goals.
