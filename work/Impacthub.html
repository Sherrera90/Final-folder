Case Study

ImpactHub

An app designed to help people discover, trust, and support nonprofits through transparency, and privacy while allowing users to make confident decisions.

Role

UX/UI Designer

 Focus

UX Strategy, UI Systems

 Year

2025

 Tools

Miro, Whimsical, Procreate, Figma

 This project is a ficticious scenario, completed as part of Entrylevel's UX Design program

Overview

ImpactHub is an platform for both individuals who are looking to volunteer and non-profits looking for volunteers. The app provides a private space for the user to explore on their own, and gives the organizations a chance to expand their volunteer base. This helps to simplify onboarding, boost engagement, track hours from both sides, all while giving the users and in-depth lookingb into the individuals and charities available.

Challenge

There are many websites out there that are designed to help users find places to volunteer, but they lack functionality, discoverability, or inconsistent information. Users asked for full disclosure regarding the organization, their policies, and where their funds are being alllocated. They also requested volunteer tracking abilities, and organization features like a calendar and hours tracked.

Solution

The goal was to create a mobile app that was functional, and easy to use while addressing all of the needs of our users. Optimizing the user interface for both large and small screens, creating functionality that transitions between devices, and enchancing accessibility to a variety of users.

The Approach

User Personas

Manuel

The Curious Explorer

"I want to explore art from my home without feeling overwhelmed by complex interfaces."

Pain Points
  • Static, non-interactive digital tours.
  • Difficulty navigating large museum databases.
Goals
  • Discover new artists easily.
  • Engage with high-quality, zoomable art assets.

Valeria

The Busy Student

"I need credible, actionable research outcomes for my history projects."

Pain Points
  • Uncertainty about source credibility.
  • Lack of clear business/educational value.
Goals
  • Access verified historical data.
  • Quickly save and cite museum exhibits.

Summary

Through thoughtful research, transparent data, and intuitive design, this app creates a simple and trustworthy way for individuals to connect with nonprofits. Features like the volunteer calendar, rating system, and funding breakdown were directly informed by real user needs and helped shape the overall experience. Working through the full process, from research to wireframes, strengthened my ability to think critically and design with empathy and accessibility. I was able to translate research into thoughtful design decisions and create clear solutions that supported user goals.
